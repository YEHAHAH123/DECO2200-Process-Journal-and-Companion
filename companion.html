Improvement of high-fidelity prototype

Improvement of our tracking page

Based on the feedback we received from our low-fidelity prototype, some people are confused about our tracing page. In the wireframe, the tracking page in our low-fidelity prototype is that we will show both the real-time tracking and the together, the user can drag the button up and down to see more tracing method they want. However, most of the respondents are confused about the button, which they mistaken as the zoom in button, and they found it not useful and distracting in the interface. In this case, we decide to make a change to the button in our tracing page.

Figure 1.1 - The improvement of our tracking page

Our team changed the button to a drag up bar, which is similar to new IOS system, it clearly indicates user to drag on and down to make the half screen full screen. Moreover, in our high-fidelity prototype, we incorporate the order list and pick-up QR code in the same page, which user will be able to change how they want to pick up the food anytime. They can also check their order details and the location of the drone at the same time.

Color and typeface

In the process of making low-fidelity prototype to high-fidelity prototype, we add pictures and colour to our prototype. According to Melissa Breyer, researchers often point to warm colors as appetite-stimulating. According to the Rohm and Haas Paint Quality Institute, yellow is an appetite-stimulating color as it is associated with energy and happiness. Therefore, we decided to make yellow color as the main color of our APP. We also use light-grey and white as complementary color of our APP and they will make our APP simple and clear when they combine together, people will also feel comfortable.

Figure 3.1 - Colors we used
Figure 3.2 - Fonts (Montserrat alternative and Montserrat)

For font, we used two sans-serif typefaces for our APP, montserrat alternative for our title and montserrat for the body. Moreover, montserrat is the top 5 font for mobile apps. (Sparklin, Top 5 UI fonts for website and mobile apps) Since we used these three colour and the same font on every page, it keeps the consistency of our APP.

Improvement of some details

For the restaurant page, at first, we put the text on the picture directly and we feel that it is unclear and confused. After we received the feedback from our tutor, we made a mask on the top of the restaurant picture and we put the text on the top of the mask and picture. We found that is more clear.

As for the button, we started with a rectangle, and then we felt that our button was a bit fake. After looking at other people's APPs, we found that many APP's buttons have shadows under the rectangle, and their buttons have the radian at their edges as well. After adding shadow for our button, we found that the rectangle looks more like a button.

User testing

After we done our high-fidelity prototype, we asked our friends to help us to test our prototype. According to the feedback, some of them feel that we lose the page of entering the credit card details. They feel that our payment process is not completed without it, so we add the card detail page.
